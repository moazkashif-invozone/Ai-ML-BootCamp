# TechGear Pro - Policies

## Refund Policy
- Full refund available within 30 days of purchase for unused products in original packaging.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
- Return shipping is free for defective items. Customers pay return shipping for non-defective returns.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Digital products and software downloads are non-refundable.

## Warranty Policy
- TechGear Pro comes with a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only. It does NOT cover:
  - Accidental damage or drops
  - Water damage (device is not waterproof)
  - Normal wear and tear
  - Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  - Damage from using non-certified chargers or cables
- To file a warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a description of the issue.

## Shipping Policy
- Free standard shipping on orders over $50 (5-7 business days).
- Express shipping available for $12.99 (2-3 business days).
- International shipping available to select countries for $25 (7-14 business days).
- Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days.
- Tracking information is emailed once the order ships.
- TechGear Pro LLC is not responsible for customs fees or import duties on international orders.

## Return Process
1. Log into your account and initiate a return from your order history.
2. Print the prepaid return label (if eligible for free return shipping).
3. Pack the item securely in original packaging.
4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location.
5. Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after receipt.

## Privacy Policy
- We collect only necessary information to process orders and provide support.
- Your data is never sold to third parties.
- We use industry-standard encryption for all transactions.
- You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting privacy@techgearpro.com.

## Cancellation Policy
- Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement, free of charge.
- After 1 hour, the order may have entered processing and cancellation is not guaranteed.
- To cancel, visit Orders in your account or contact support immediately.
