# TechGear Pro - Frequently Asked Questions

## What is the TechGear Pro?
TechGear Pro is a premium all-in-one wireless charging station and device organizer. It supports simultaneous charging of up to 3 devices (phone, watch, earbuds) with 15W fast charging capability.

## How do I set up my TechGear Pro?
1. Unbox the device and remove all packaging materials.
2. Connect the USB-C power cable to the back of the charging station.
3. Plug the power adapter into a wall outlet.
4. Place your phone on the main charging pad (marked with a green LED indicator).
5. Place your watch on the top cradle and earbuds on the designated spot.
6. The LED indicators will turn blue when charging begins.

## What devices are compatible with TechGear Pro?
TechGear Pro is compatible with all Qi-certified devices including iPhone 12 and newer, Samsung Galaxy S20 and newer, Google Pixel 6 and newer, Apple Watch Series 4 and newer, and most wireless earbud cases.

## How long does it take to fully charge a device?
- Phone: approximately 1.5 hours (0-80% in 45 minutes with fast charging)
- Watch: approximately 1 hour
- Earbuds: approximately 45 minutes
Charging times may vary based on device model and battery condition.

## Is the TechGear Pro travel-friendly?
Yes. The device weighs 340g and measures 20cm x 10cm x 4cm. It comes with a travel pouch and a cable management system. However, the power adapter is not included in international versions.

## Can I use my phone while it's charging on TechGear Pro?
Yes, the charging pad is designed with a non-slip surface and angled stand that allows you to use your phone's face ID and view notifications while charging.

## What color options are available?
TechGear Pro comes in three colors: Midnight Black, Arctic White, and Ocean Blue.

## What is the warranty period?
TechGear Pro comes with a standard 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty plans are available for purchase within 30 days of the original purchase.

## How do I clean my TechGear Pro?
Use a soft, dry microfiber cloth to wipe the surface. Do not use any liquid cleaners, alcohol, or abrasive materials. Unplug the device before cleaning.

## Does TechGear Pro support fast charging?
Yes, it supports up to 15W fast charging for compatible devices. Fast charging is available on the primary phone charging pad only. The watch and earbuds charging spots support standard 5W charging.
